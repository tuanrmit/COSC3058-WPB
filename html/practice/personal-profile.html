Background

Tuan obtained his Diploma in 2005 and Bachelor of Science in 2012 in Computer Science majoring in IT Service Science from Auckland University of Technology, and his Master and Ph.D. in Information Science in learning analytics from Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology in 2019 and 2022. From 2007-2009, he serviced in Vietnam Military. From 2017-2022, he was a laboratory assistant, research assistant, and teaching assistant at JAIST, and continued as Lecturer at RMIT University since 2023.

Research interests

Tuan's research explores the relationship between cognition and metacognition of human mind that reveals when we learn to know the world.
